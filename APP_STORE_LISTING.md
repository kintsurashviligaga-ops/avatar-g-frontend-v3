# MyAvatar — App Store Connect Listing (copy-paste ready)

All copy below is written to be **accurate to shipped features** (image, video, music,
lip-sync, chat, voice) and **App Store compliant**: no mention of Stripe/web purchases
(anti-steering), no trademarked keywords (e.g. no "ChatGPT"/"Midjourney" — Apple rejects those).

Character limits are App Store Connect's. Georgian/Russian count per-character in UTF-16;
the ASC editor shows a live counter — verify the tight fields (Name, Subtitle, Keywords,
Promo) there before saving.
